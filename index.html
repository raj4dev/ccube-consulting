"The difference between stupidity and genius is that genius has its limits." - Albert Einstein

C-Cube offers comprehensive business and marketing services to technology companies to help determine their strategy and execution in security, Web services/service-oriented architecture, and wireless markets.

C-Cube provides services to startups and established companies in meeting their needs in business development, outboard marketing, product marketing, and product management strategy. These include developing go-to-market plans, competitive positioning, marketing collateral, FAQs, product brochures, sales briefs, and whitepapers.

Our founders have over 50 years of combined professional experience with Amdahl, AOL/Netscape Security Solutions, Attachmate, Cray Research, Infravio, Logictier, Motorola, nCircle, Netscape, Siemens, Sun Microsystems, and Wyse Technology.
